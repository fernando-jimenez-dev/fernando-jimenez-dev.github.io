# Content Style Guide

## Voice

- Calm, technical, and precise.
- Practical over hype.
- Clear and direct, not performative.

## Writing Principles

- Clarity over cleverness.
- Specific claims over generic adjectives.
- Explain tradeoffs where relevant.
- Prefer short paragraphs and readable rhythm.

## Positioning Language

Use phrasing aligned with current positioning:

- "Staff-Level .NET Engineer"
- "Practical Architect"
- "Systems-minded"
- "Pragmatic automation"

Avoid:

- Overstated marketing language
- Trend-chasing language
- Vague buzzword clusters

## Page-Level Guidance

Homepage:

- Quick orientation and credibility.
- Strong but concise introduction.

About:

- Long-form narrative is acceptable.
- Keep transitions clear and intentional.

Future writing:

- Long-form, analytical, tradeoff-focused.
- No shallow listicle style.

## SEO and Metadata

- Keep titles concise and descriptive.
- Keep descriptions factual, specific, and non-clickbait.
- Keep canonical/open graph values aligned with page purpose.
